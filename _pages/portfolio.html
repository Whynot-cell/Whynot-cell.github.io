Large-diameter, extra-long energy pile field tests based on DFOS

07/2023 - present

- Supported by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (No. 41572271).

- Proposed an inter-calibrated DFOS method to enhance the accuracy and reliabilty of DFOS monitoring.

- Researched the novel thermo-mechanical response of a large-diameter, extra-long energy pile.

Research on the estimation of seepage field using DFOS temperature profiles of energy piles

04/2024 - present

- Developed distributed temperature sensors based on Optical Frequency Domain Reflectometer (OFDR).

- Evaluation of seepage fields using energy pile temperature variations during cooling/heating operations.

Research on the thermal performance of PHC energy piles backfilled with phase change composites

04/2023 - 12/2023

- Proposed a theoretical model to calculate the thermal properties of the phase change composites (paraffin and graphite).

- Researched the dynamic competitive relationship between paraffin and graphite within the phase change composites.

Research on the glacier evolution study based on DFOS

06/2024 - present

- Establishment of Glacier DFOS monitoring stations to obtain key data on glacier accumulation and ablation

- Calculation of glacial heat fluxes and research on ice-gas interfaces
